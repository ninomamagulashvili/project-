    Breakfast

    Breakfast is served every morning from 8 till noon.The buffet breakfast features a wide variety of traditional breakfast dishes as well as Georgian alternatives: Georgian yogurt "matsoni", fresh homemade pastry, fresh fruit and vegetables, cereal, nuts & dried fruits, jams and much more.

    Rooftop Terrace

    Amante's rooftop terrace with a panoramic city view serves a wide variety of drinks and cocktails every day from noon till 3:00 AM. This is the perfect spot to hold small gatherings and organize celebrations like birthdays or small intimate weddings.This is also where we host outdoor yoga and pilates sessions.

    Concierge Service

    Our team is always ready to assist our guests with any request, might it be restaurant reservations, tickets to shows, transportation arrangements, tour organization, etc.
