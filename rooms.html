    Amor room

    This room was designed to be the most romantic, hence the name "Amor". It comes with a king size bed and a bathtub right in the bedrooom. The room also has two balconies with panoramic city views - one is French, in front of the bathtub and the other is large with chairs and a table. The guests can enjoy all the usual amenities, plus a complimentary bottle of Georgian wine. Room Details ...

    View Double or Twin

    View Double or Twin rooms are located on higher floors and all have big balconies with spectacular city views. The area is 20 sq. meters. With advance notice guests can choose to have either one double or two single beds arranged. The bathroom is equipped with all essential amenities, bathrobe, sleepers and hairdryer. Rooms also come with complimentary coffee and tea facilities, minibar, safe deposit box, table and chair, flat screen TV, clothes rack, access to Wi-Fi. Room Details ...
